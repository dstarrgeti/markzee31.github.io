Nice To Meet You

Born and raised in Columbus, Ohio, I have a strong connection with this city and helping maintain our power as "the biggest little city" in the country. Upon graduation from Saint Charles Prep, I left town to attend Marshall University in Huntington, WV. I played lacrosse and received my BBA in Economics and Marketing.

After five years of being an accountant, I decided I needed a change. After a year of training from an established developer, I decided to attend the Web Development program at Columbus State. While in school, I began working on sites for small businesses in Columbus, Phoenix and Denver. Upon completion of the in-class course work at Columbus State, I started an internship at The Shipyard, a design agency located in Columbus, OH.

At The Shipyard, I've honed my existing skills and have expanded my abilities exponentially. My experience, confidence in my skills and abilities have been on the fast-track since joining the dev team at The Shipyard. Feel free to contact me here with any work inquiries or questions.
